Workshops

Structured workshops designed for students, parents, teams, and workplaces. These sessions focus on discipline, identity, and building the habits needed to perform, lead, and take control. Built from lived experience, not theory, each workshop delivers practical frameworks that can be applied immediately.

What It Is

Built By Dav workshops go deeper than a one-off talk. They are in smaller groups with more interaction letting every person in the room leave with something specific and practical they are committing to. These workshops are available for students, parents and workplace teams.

Student Workshops

A half-day interactive session for a small group of students identified by the school as struggling socially, emotionally or with confidence

What's Covered

  • Identity mapping, who you are now versus who you're deciding to become.
  • Finding your one thing, the daily habit that belongs entirely to you.
  • The Discipline Metrics framework, three questions that rebuild identity from the inside out.
  • A public commitment made in front of the group, the single most powerful accountability tool.

Parent Workshops

A single evening session for parents of young people going through adversity. Practical, honest and built on real experience.

What's Covered

  • What bullying actually does to your child's identity that nobody talks about.
  • Why common responses often make it worse.
  • How to have the conversation when they've shut down completely.
  • A practical plan to support them at home.
  • Three specific commitments every parent leaves with tonight.

Workplace Workshops

A keynote or interactive workshop for corporate teams focused on resilience, identity and building through adversity

What's Covered

  • How adversity shapes identity in professional environments.
  • Building resilience that lasts beyond one rough period.
  • Practical discipline tools teams can implement immediately.
  • Interactive elements tailored to your team and organisation.

What Every Workshop Includes

  • Tailored content for your specific audience.
  • Interactive activities, not just listening.
  • Real stories, no scripts.
  • A practical framework applied immediately in the room.
  • Every person leaves with a specific written commitment.
